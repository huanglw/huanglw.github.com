关于flex布局父容器属性实例

flex-direction:column(主轴方向为竖直方向)

.father{
    display:flex;
    justify-content: space-around;
    align-items:center/flex-start/flex-end;
    flex-direction:column;
    border:5px dashed white;
    height:400px;
    width:400px;
}
.son{
    display:flex;
    justify-content: center;
    align-items:center;
    height:100px;
    border:2px solid white;
    width:100px;
    font-weight:bolder;
    font-size:16px;
    color:white;
}
